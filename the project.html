OUR PRODUCT

Masks

The masks are made from refined wood, and designed around the specifications from clients.

The specifications include measurements; ear to ear as well as chin to forhead.

There are different products as well. We have two different types of masks: a half mask and a full mask. You can choose to have it painted. We also have keychains and necklaces. All of this is specified in our Patron.

how to submit

Clients can send in masks through either our social media pages or to our website. clients may leave the design up to us or they can submit a picture of their design for our employees to work off of.

Patrons must include measurements and an address to send mask to.
