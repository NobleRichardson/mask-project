OUR PRODUCT

Masks

The masks are made from refined wood, and designed around sent in specifications from clients.

specifications include measurements; ear to ear as well as chin to forhead.

how to submit

clients can send in masks through either our social media pages and or to our website. clients may leave design up to us or they can submit a picture of their design for our employees to work off of.

patrons must include measurements and an address to send mask to.
